S'mores Recipe

Description

S'mores are a summer favorite, great for a cookout with friends.

With a few simple and cheap ingredients and a campfire, you can have a fantastic dessert.

Ingredients

  • Graham Crackers
  • Large Marshmallows
  • Chocolate bars (e.g. Hersheys)
  • Skewers to roast the marshmallows

Directions

  1. Prepare your smore by taking two graham cracker pieces, placing a square of chocolate on one, and placing a marshmallow on a skewer.
  2. Hold your marshmallow over the fire, slowly rotating until it reaches a golden color
  3. Remove your marshmallow from the skewer, and place on the graham cracker you prepared without the chocolate square.
  4. Combine graham cracker pieces so chocolate and marshmallow are in the middle, eat like a sandwich.
  5. Enjoy!
