Fruit Salad

Description

Fruit salad is an easy to make, refreshing dish that is great for a summer day.

Just choose your favorite fruits and determine how many people you're serving when deciding just how much to buy.

Ingredients

  • Grapes
  • Strawberries
  • Bananas
  • Berries
  • Any other fruit you would like to add!

Directions

  1. Cut all fruit into small pieces
  2. Add fruit to a bowl.
  3. Mix the fruit so that a scoop delivers a variety of fruit.
  4. Serve!
