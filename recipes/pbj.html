Peanut Butter & Jelly Recipe

Description

Peanut Butter and Jelly sandwiches are a classic in American lunch boxes. They are cheap, simple, and tasty.

With only three indredients, none of which require any preperation, the PB&J sandwich is great for beginners.

Ingredients

  • Bread (2 slices)
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly (any fruit flavor)

Directions

  1. Open peanut butter and jelly jars.
  2. Take bread out of bag, and place two slices on a plate.
  3. With a knife, spread peanut butter on one side of one slice of bread.
  4. With a knife, spread jelly on one side of the other slice of bread.
  5. Push the two pieces of bread together so that the peanut butter and jelly are in the middle.
  6. With the knife, slice the sandwich down the middle.
  7. Enjoy.
