Lasagna

About the Recipe

Lasagna is a classic family dinner dish. It's simple ingredients wise, filling, and delicious.

The following ingredients list and directions are sourced from All Recipes, please visit their page to give a view and read the recipe's story.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Italian sausage
  • 3/4 lb lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic crushed
  • 1 28 ounce can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 6.5 ounce cans tomato sauce
  • 2 6 ounce cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons fresh parsley chopped
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounce ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 lb mozzarella cheese (sliced)
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Cook the meats, onion, and garlic together at medium heat until browned.
  3. Mix in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and water.
  4. Mix in the sugar, 2 tablesppons parsley, 1 teaspoon salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper.
  5. Simmer, covered, for about 90 minutes. Stir occassionally.
  6. Cook the lasagna noodles in salted boiling water based on the box instructions. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  7. Combine the ricotta, egg, 2 tablespoons parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt in a mixing bowl.
  8. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  9. Complete two rounds of the following layers:
    1. 1 1/2 cups meat sauce
    2. 6 noodles lenghtwise (overlapping slightly
    3. 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture
    4. 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices
    5. 1 1/2 cups meat sauce
    6. Sprinkle of 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
  10. Top with the remaining mozzarella and parmesan cheese.
  11. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for another 25 minutes.
  12. Remove from oven, let rest for 15 minutes, serve.
